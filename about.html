Welcome to Bookbazar

let's go!

Bookbazar, the leading Swedish publisher of Science and Technology, publishes training books for students and professionals of all levels. Leader in the fields of business books and applied sciences, it also offers an essential catalog for the psychology and social action markets. Finally, for twenty years, its scientific culture fund has been among the most popular among booksellers. Bookbazar is also an important player in personal development under the InterÉditions brand and in paramedics under the Édiscience brand. Bookbazar also houses a catalog specializing in Human and Social Sciences: its major learning (“128”, “Cursus”) and deepening (“U”, “Lettres Sup”, “Perspectives géopolitiques”, etc.) collections are supplemented by a wide range of resource books (atlases, annuals) and essays (collections "Elements of response", "Time for ideas" ...). and finally a large collection of different book categories
